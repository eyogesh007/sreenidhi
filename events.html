Events

Traditional day

Traditional day is the day when every Sreenidhian embraces the desi in them and come out dressed in their best desi avatars. Everyone looks forward to this grand day of getting dressed and capturing beautiful memories in their fabulous outfits that they can cherish for the rest of their lives. The main event starts in the evening when all the cultural performances take place in Naach. The dance and music performances along with other exhilarating performances bring life to this evening. It is the last event of the semester and it sure does end with a bam!

Rigolade

It is one of the most anticipated fests of the year conducted in collaboration with the Arts Club and TechVision Club, a very well-known clubs for its cultural and wide range of creative domains. It gives numerous opportunities for students to showcase their talents, ranging from technical to cultural fields. It includes various technical and non-technical events showcased from clubs across the college and ends with one of the most awaited events of the fest, the cult night.

Graduation cermony

Graduation marks a milepost in the lives of student. It's a serious yet joyful event. And therein lies the conundrum

Dandiya Night

One of the best and lively dandiya night packed with loads of entertainment. The event starts with a mellifluous performance by "Sargam band". Sreenidhians unleash their energy as a dancer by grooving to the terrific beats. Two days of food festival is a great spread to savour the delicacies sold on the campus. The students also participate in the fun filled events conducted by the arts club like singing, dancing, auctions etc., It is a great platform to celebrate you as an artist.
