Department of MBA

Vision

  • To pursue academic excellence by establishing linkages with Industry and advanced institutions at National and International level for enhancing teaching, Research and Development activities and to promote Entrepreneurship; to be rated in the top 20 business schools in the country.

Mission

  • To prepare innovative and ethical leaders who understand the impact of business on society and create value for the world.
  • To make our students comprehend organizational systems, the vital role of leadership, and the forces transforming the global economy.
  • We provide scholarly knowledge and insights that advance management practice through our research, teaching, and community engagement.
